Microsoft Bookings – Appointment Booking System

Provider and Data Processing Agreement

For online appointment booking, we use Microsoft Bookings, a service of Microsoft Ireland Operations Limited, One Microsoft Place, South County Business Park, Leopardstown, Dublin 18, D18 P521, Ireland (“Microsoft”).

Microsoft processes the data described below on our behalf. We have concluded a Data Processing Agreement (DPA) with Microsoft that ensures compliance with the General Data Protection Regulation (GDPR).

Data Processed During Appointment Booking

When booking an appointment through Microsoft Bookings, the following data is processed:

  • First name, last name
  • Email address
  • Phone number (optional)
  • Company (optional)
  • Selected appointment (date and time)
  • Appointment description/concern (optional)
  • Technical data (IP address, device type, browser)

Purpose of Processing

Data processing serves exclusively for appointment coordination, confirmation, and execution as well as contact before and after the appointment.

Legal Basis

Processing is based on:

  • Art. 6(1)(b) GDPR (contract initiation), when the appointment booking serves to prepare a specific order.
  • Art. 6(1)(f) GDPR (legitimate interest), when the appointment booking serves for general consultation or an initial meeting. Our legitimate interest consists in efficient appointment coordination and avoidance of double bookings.
